Consent and transparency

No hidden extraction

Every public form must explain what is collected, why it is collected, who can access it, how it may be used, how long it may be kept, and how someone can withdraw, correct, or avoid sensitive disclosure.

No Community of Kindness fundraiser, Ko-fi, PayPal, Princess Dragonblood, story submission, or participation form should go live until the release checklist has been reviewed.
