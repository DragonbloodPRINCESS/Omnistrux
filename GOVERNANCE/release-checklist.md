# Release Checklist

Use this checklist before publishing any page, form, campaign, fundraiser, support pathway, story edit, AI function, or public claim.

A release is not ready if any answer is no.

## Required Checks

1. Authorship
   - Does this correctly preserve Kristal Cornwell as source, author, or decision-holder where appropriate?
   - Does it distinguish AI-assisted drafting or structuring from human authorship?

2. Truth Framing
   - Is every meaningful claim framed as verified evidence, self-stated testimony, corroborated material, interpretation, creative work, technical proposal, or open enquiry?
   - Are symbolic, spiritual, civic, technical, and factual claims kept distinct?

3. Consent
   - Does every affected person understand and control what they are contributing?
   - Is consent scoped, informed, revocable where reasonably possible, and recorded where needed?

4. Privacy
   - Have sensitive identities, hardship details, locations, faces, number plates, contact details, and community vulnerabilities been protected?
   - Is the data collected the minimum needed for the stated purpose?

5. Dignity
   - Does this represent people as agents rather than pity objects or conversion assets?
   - Does it avoid exploiting homelessness, hardship, trauma, or community care for attention?

6. Practicality
   - Does this help someone take a useful action, understand the story, offer appropriate support, register interest, or make a clear decision?

7. Continuity
   - Is the release connected to the correct ecosystem module without collapsing separate projects into one confusing claim?

8. Exportability
   - Can Kristal retain, access, export, and move the underlying content, submissions, contact records, consent records, and version history?

9. Accountability
   - Is there a traceable record of decisions, permissions, approvals, source materials, edits, and release status?

10. Integrity
   - Would Kristal recognise this as aligned with what the work is actually for: dignity made practical?

## Community Support Gate

Do not publish donation, volunteer, goods, meal, sponsorship, or Community of Kindness pathways as authorised until approval, recipient details, fund handling, reporting, and contact/accountability language are confirmed.

## Intake Form Gate

Do not publish forms collecting lived-experience stories, support needs, identity details, hardship information, or sensitive community data until the form states:

- what is collected
- why it is collected
- who can access it
- how it will be used
- how long it may be kept
- whether public use is optional
- how withdrawal or correction can be requested
- how to participate without disclosing sensitive information
