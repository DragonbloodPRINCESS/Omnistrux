# Community Support Approval Needed

Community of Kindness / Freya support pathways must not be published as authorised until explicit approval and accountability details are confirmed.

This includes any GoFundMe presented as supporting Freya's Community of Kindness / Community of Kindness Lunchies.

## Why This Gate Exists

The Walyalup Bridge Story can meaningfully point readers toward practical community care, but connection is not ownership. This repository must not imply formal partnership, endorsement, fundraising authority, governance authority, or permission before those facts are confirmed.

## Required Before Publishing Community GoFundMe CTAs

Confirm and record:

- approved recipient name
- verified GoFundMe URL
- who gave approval
- what support pathway is approved
- whether donations, goods, meals, volunteering, updates, sponsorship, or partnerships are included
- who handles funds or goods
- how support will be reported or acknowledged
- who supporters can contact with questions
- what language is approved for public use
- whether the approval is time-limited or conditional

## Creator Support Separation

Kristal's Ko-fi, PayPal, and Princess Dragonblood links may appear as creator or creative-persona support CTAs only when they are clearly labelled as separate from Community of Kindness donations.

Required separation language:

> Ko-fi and PayPal support Kristal Cornwell's creative work, writing, music, recovery, continuity, and the development of this ecosystem. These are separate from Community of Kindness donations.

Princess Dragonblood should be framed as Kristal's music and creative persona, not as a community fundraiser.

## Allowed Before Approval

A holding statement may say:

> Approved community support pathways are being confirmed. Until then, this site will not publish donation or support instructions on behalf of any person, group, or initiative.

Creator support links may still be used if they are labelled separately and do not imply Community of Kindness benefit.

## Not Allowed Before Approval

Do not publish:

- Community of Kindness GoFundMe links presented as authorised without approval
- claims of partnership or endorsement
- use of another person's community work to legitimise unrelated projects
- photos or identifying material involving vulnerable people without consent
- fund handling claims that cannot be traced
- urgency language that pressures donors before accountability is clear
- Ko-fi or PayPal buttons that could be mistaken for Community of Kindness donations

## Release Gate

This file and `PUBLICATIONS/cta-framework.md` must be reviewed before any Community of Kindness, Freya, donation, meal support, volunteer, goods, sponsorship, fundraiser, Ko-fi, PayPal, or Princess Dragonblood CTA goes live.
