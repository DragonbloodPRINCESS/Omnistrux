# Ecosystem Map

The Diamond Soul Constellation launch is a connected but modular ecosystem. Each part must remain understandable on its own while linking into the wider architecture.

## Public Entry

### Walyalup Bridge Story

Role: primary public entry point.

Purpose:

- Publish lived testimony in an accessible, dignified reading format.
- Connect personal experience to structural understanding.
- Invite practical care, participation, sharing, and civic attention.
- Avoid pity marketing and emotional extraction.

Truth mode: self-stated testimony, civic reflection, and interpretive systems insight.

## Immediate Care Connection

### Community of Kindness Connection

Role: possible practical support pathway.

Purpose:

- Point readers toward existing community care only where permission and accountability are confirmed.
- Make support pathways transparent: donation, meals, goods, volunteering, updates, and partnership interest.

Release gate:

- Do not publish as an authorised fundraiser or formal partnership until approval, recipient details, fund handling, reporting, and accountability language are documented.

## Participation Layer

### Living Layers

Role: consent-first registration and participation capture.

Purpose:

- Let people safely register their relationship to the community and desired level of involvement.
- Capture interest categories without requiring sensitive disclosure.
- Prepare future community mapping, story capture, resource mapping, support pathways, and collaboration.

Initial status: MVP interest and registration page, not a full app.

## Future Civic and Creative Layer

### TimeShift Freo / TimeShiftAR

Role: future-facing place-based cultural, civic, creative, and AR pathway.

Purpose:

- Invite collaborators, artists, historians, residents, businesses, funders, developers, and civic partners.
- Position Walyalup Bridge as one possible living narrative layer in a wider place-based memory ecosystem.
- Test interest before building a full AR platform.

Initial status: interest capture and concept page, not a finished app.

## Coherent Framework

### Diamond Soul Constellation

Role: plain-language public architecture.

Purpose:

- Connect story, care, place, creativity, participation, ethical technology, and civic imagination.
- Keep the first public launch practical and intelligible.
- Offer deeper conceptual layers later for people who opt in.

Suggested public framing:

> The Diamond Soul Constellation is a growing framework for connecting real human experience with practical community care, ethical technology, creative storytelling, and better civic design. It begins here, with one lived story, one community response, and one invitation to help build something kinder.

## Governance and Infrastructure

### OmniStrux

Role: governance, structure, and operational coordination layer.

Purpose:

- Define modules, permissions, workflows, releases, roles, evidence handling, and future expansion.

Initial status: repository and governance foundation.

### CareX

Role: consent, privacy, reciprocity, and ethical data principles.

Purpose:

- Ensure participation is transparent, voluntary, beneficial, and non-extractive.
- Define what data is collected, why, who can access it, and how it may be withdrawn or corrected.

Initial status: principles applied to forms and release gates.

## Later Modules

Later work may include Axtelia, Plus One, creative commerce, archive exploration, AI continuity tools, AR experiences, CareX dashboards, or deeper OmniStrux systems. These must not crowd the first public launch.

## First Launch Rule

Lead with lived story, practical care, consent, and plain language. Reveal the wider constellation gradually.
